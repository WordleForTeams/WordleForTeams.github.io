PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information when you use the Wordle App.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information from users of the Wordle App. No user names, email addresses, or any other personal data is collected.

2. Use of Cookies

The Wordle App does not use cookies or similar technologies for tracking user activity.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any data collected from users with third parties.

4. Data Security

As we do not collect user data, there are no specific security measures in place. However, we are committed to ensuring the security of any information that may be collected in the future.

5. Age Restriction

The Wordle App does not have any age restrictions. It is open to users of all age groups.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

Users are encouraged to visit the Wordle App's website to check for any changes to the Privacy Policy. Any updates will be posted on the website.
